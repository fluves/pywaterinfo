Timeseriesgroup_ids

Introduction

For a number of time series, the VMM and HIC provide the group identifiers in the online available documentation. These identifiers are the preferred source to query the enlisted variables.

Note

When you only require yearly aggregations, check for a time series with a corresponding frequency to overcome large data queries as much as possible.
